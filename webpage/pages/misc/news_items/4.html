Battlefield 4 Pages Return! June 12, 2020



Up next in the list of classic Battlefield titles, Battlefield 4 returns with weapon charts, weapon comparison, and a weapon mechanics page. Battlefield 3 and Battlefield 1 have also retroactively been given their own weapon mechanics pages.
