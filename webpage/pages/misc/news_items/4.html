Battlefield 4 Pages Return! June 12, 2020



Up next in the list of classic Battlefield titles, Battlefield 4 returns with weapon charts, weapon comparison, and a weapon mechanics page. Battlefield 3 and Battlefield 1 have also retroactively been given their own weapon mechanics pages. Like the Battlefield 3 pages, the new Battlefield 4 pages can be found under the "Other Titles" category.

Thanks to Robenter for creating the weapon charts, and also to Miffyli for getting the BF4 data up to speed. Expect to see another addition to Sym.gg soon!
