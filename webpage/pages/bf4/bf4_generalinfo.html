Battlefield 4 General Information

Gameplay Mechanics
Objectives and Tickets
  • Tickets are lost upon player respawn.
  • Flags are not captured unless the capturing team has the player majority within the capzone.
Health and Health Recovery
  • Player health is 100 hp
    • In hardcore gamemodes, the default is 60 hp
    • Health and bullet damage may be different on custom preset servers

  • Players die when their health reaches zero.
    • A player will still be alive as long as health is greater than zero, now matter how minuscule the value
